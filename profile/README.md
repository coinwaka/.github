# Coinwaka

Coinwaka is a crypto payments and digital asset software platform built for Kenya and emerging markets.

Coinwaka focuses on digital asset access, wallets, merchant payments, developer APIs, payment workflows, and secure transaction infrastructure.

Coinwaka is operated by Chainwaka Technologies, Business No. BN-OVS6J37W, a business name registered in Kenya.

Business registration confirms the legal registration of the operator. It is not a CBK, CMA, or VASP licence.

## Repository policy

Public repositories under this organization are for documentation, examples, SDKs, integrations, and developer resources.

Private backend systems, secrets, keys, internal infrastructure, and production credentials must never be published here.
